HEALTHY BODY CATEGORY DEFINITIONS
Fruits and Vegetables: How many fruits or vegetables do you eat everyday?
- In a typical day, averaging workdays and weekends
- On a scale of 0 (none) to 5 (servings per day)

Daily Steps: How many steps (in thousands) do you typically walk everyday?
- Thousand steps, daily average over multiple days including work days and weeknd
- On a scale of 1 (less than 1,000) to 10 (thousand steps)

BMI Range: What is your body mass index (BMI) range?
- Your body mass index in kg divided by the square of your height in meters
- On a binary scale of 1 (below 25) to 2 (above 25)

Sleep Hours: How well do you complete your weekly to-do lists?
- Include your weekly goals, work, and personal related tasks
- On a scale of 0 (not at all) to 10 (very well) 

HEALTHY MIND CATEGORY DEFINITIONS
Daily Stress: How much stress do you typically experience everyday?
-  At work or at home, due to the environment (noise, pollution, insecurity...), your co-workers or boss, or because of tragic events such as divorce, job loss, serious illness, loss of family or friends,...
- On average over 12 months 
- On a scale of 0 (not much stress) to 5 (a lot of stress) for daily preceived level 

Daily Shouting: How often do you shout or sulk at somebody?
- In a typical week
- Expressing negative emotions in an active (shouting) or passive (sulking) manner
- On a scale of 0 (never) to 10 (or more times per week)

Flow: In a typical day, how many hours do you experience "flow"?
- 'Flow' is defined as the mental state in which you are fully immersed in performing an activity. If fully immersed, you will experience a feeling of energized focus, full involvement, and enjoyment in the process of the activity (see the YouTube video 'Flow, the secret to happiness' from Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi 
- On a scale of 0 (none) to 10 (or more hours per day)

Time for Passion: How many hours do you spend everyday doing what you are passionate about?
- Daily hours spend doing what you are passionate and dreaming about, and/or contributing to a greater cause: health, education, peace, society development, etc...
- On a scale of 0 (none) to 10 (hours)

Weekly Meditation: In a typical week, how many times do you have the opportunity to think about yourself?
- Include meditation, praying, and relaxation activities such as fitness, walking in a park, or lunch breaks

EXPERTISE CATEGORY DEFINITIONS
To-Do Completed: How well do you complete your to-do lists?
- Include weekly goals, work- and personal-related tasks
- On a scale of 0 (not at all) to 10 (very well)

Personal Awards: How many recognitions have you reveived in your life?
- Significant public recognitions validating a pero=sonal level of expertise and engagement
- E.g. diploma, degree, certificate, accreditation, award, prize, published book, presentation at major conference, medals, cups, titles...
- On a scale of 0 (none) to 10 (recognitions or more)

Achievements: How many remarkable achievements are you proud of?
- Over the last 12 months
- Include persomal achievements known to your family known to your family, close friends, or co-workers, such as running a marathon or important race, birth, successful kids, new house or major renovation, major success at work, opening a new business,...

CONNECTION CATEGORY DEFINITIONS  
Places Visited: How many new places do you visit?
- Over a period of 12 months
- Include new states and new cities as well as museums, places of interest, and parks in your neighborhood
- On a scale of 0 (none) to 10 (new places or more)

Social Network: How many people do you interact with during a typical day?
- True interactions and dialogues at home, at work, at the gym,...
- Average of workdays and weekends
- On a scale of 0 (none) to 10 (people or more)

Core Circle: How many people are very close to you?
- i.e. close family and friends ready to provide you with a long-term unconditional support
- on a scale of 0 (none) to 10 (people or more)

MEANING CATEGORY DEFINITIONS
Lost Vacation Time: How many days of vacation do you typically lose every year?
- Vacation days that are unused, lost, or carried over into the following year
- Or because of work stress during your vacation
- On a scale of 0 (none) to 10 (vacation days)

Supporting Others: How many people do you help achieve a better life?
- A reflection of your altruism or selflessness (see the TED video from Matthieu Ricard 'How to let altruism be your guide')
- e.g. caring for your family, actively supporting a friend, mentoring, coaching, developing or promoting a co-worker,...
- Over a period of 12 months
- On a scale of 0 (none) to 10 (people or more)

Life Vision: For how many years ahead is your life vision very clear for?
- e.g. illustrated on a vision board, detailed in a personal journal, or openly discussed with your spouse or close friends
- On a scale of 0 (I do not have a life vision) to 10 (years or more)

Donation: How many times do you donate your time or money to good causes?
- Over a period of 12 months
- Include financial donation, your time contribution, fundraising, volunteering, serving your country or the poor,...
- On a scale of 0 (none) to 5 (or more)

Sufficient Income: How sufficient is your income to cover basic life expenses?
- Such as costs of housing, food, health care, car, and education
- On a binary scale of 1 (not or hardly sufficent) to 2 (sufficient)
